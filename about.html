About LMSTFY Prank

This website is a fun, interactive prank inspired by Let Me Google That For You. It simulates typing your search query automatically and then “searches” it, giving the illusion that the site is doing all the work for you. The goal is purely humorous and nostalgic, replicating the old-school style of web pranks that gently poke fun at people who ask obvious questions online.

Everything is handled automatically, from typing to simulating the button click, and then redirecting to the chosen search engine. It’s a playful experiment in web interactivity and timing effects, designed for entertainment and a bit of mischief.
